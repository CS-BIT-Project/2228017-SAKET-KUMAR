Exploring Bodh Gaya: The Spiritual Heart of Bihar

Bodh Gaya, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment, is a must-visit for spiritual seekers. The Mahabodhi Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, stands as a testament to Bihar's rich history.

Nalanda: The Ancient Seat of Learning

Discover the ruins of Nalanda University, one of the world's oldest universities. This historical site offers a glimpse into Bihar's intellectual past.

Rajgir: A Blend of Nature and History

Rajgir’s hot springs, Vishwa Shanti Stupa, and ancient ruins make it a unique destination for tourists and history enthusiasts alike.
