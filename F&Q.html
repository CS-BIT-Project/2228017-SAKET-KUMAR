Frequently Asked Questions

What is OneTour.Planner?

OneTour.Planner is a travel planning platform that helps you explore destinations, book packages, and customize your trips to places like Bihar, offering a seamless experience with curated tourist spots, hotels, and more.

How do I book a package?

You can book a package by selecting your destination, dates, and preferences in the Packages section, then clicking "Send Bill" to finalize via WhatsApp or contacting our support team.

Are the prices customizable?

Yes, you can adjust the price range using the filters on the Packages page, selecting options between ₹3000 and ₹20000 based on your budget.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently facilitate payments through secure WhatsApp transactions after you send your bill. More payment options will be added soon!
