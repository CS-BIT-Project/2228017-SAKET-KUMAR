BIHAR

A sanctuary for the soul. In the heart of India,
the state of Bihar beckons Travellers with its
history, spirituality, and natural beauty. This
ancient land is the birthplace of two of the
world's major religions, Buddhism and Jainism,
offering pilgrims and history enthusiasts an
unparalleled journey through time.
